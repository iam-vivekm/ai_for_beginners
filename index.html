Using AI Responsibly

AI is powerful, so we need to use it wisely!

✅ Do:

• Use AI to help you learn and understand better

• Always check if AI information is correct

• Give credit when AI helps you with homework

• Use AI to spark your creativity


❌ Don't:

• Copy AI work without understanding it

• Rely only on AI for important decisions

• Share personal information with AI systems

• Use AI to cheat on tests
